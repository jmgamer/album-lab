Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band

The Beatles

Released May 26, 1967 in the UK

This album was the Beatles' eighth studio album. It was the number one album in the UK for 27 weeks and in the US for 15 weeks. It's major features were innovative recording techniques and an interesting album cover, which won the album four Grammy Awards in 1968 - Best Album Cover, Graphic Arts; Album of the Year; Best Engineered Album; and Best Contemporary Album.

Band Members

Paul McCartney - Lead Vocals
